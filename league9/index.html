Rules & League Settings

📊 Minimum Weekly Scores

This league is currently set to 5 scores. Only your best 5 scores each week count toward your weekly total (Monday–Sunday). If you play all 7 days, your 2 highest (worst) scores are thrown out. Once a player reaches the minimum 5 scores for the week, their row will be highlighted to indicate they are eligible for a weekly score.

You need at least 5 scores in a week to compete. The league manager can adjust this setting:

🏆 Season Wins

The current season goal is 4 weekly wins. The first player to reach that number is crowned the Season Champion. Leagues running in Division Mode use a shorter target of 3 wins per division for a faster-paced season.

Configurable season win targets are coming in a future update—stay tuned!

🕐 Window to Post

Each day's Wordle score can be submitted between 12:00 AM and 11:59 PM Pacific Time. Only the current day's Wordle puzzle number is accepted—scores from previous days cannot be posted retroactively. The scoring week runs Monday through Sunday.

❌ Fails Don't Count

If you fail a Wordle (X/6), it does not count as one of your used scores and won't affect your weekly total. Failed attempts will appear in the Failed column of the weekly table for reference. Failed attempts do count as a 7 in your All-Time stats.

🤝 Ties Share the Win

If two or more players finish the week with the same total score, they all earn a weekly win. The same applies to the season—if multiple players reach 4 weekly wins at the same time, they share the Season Victory.

First player to reach 4 weekly wins is crowned Season Champion!

⚔️ Division Mode (Available)

Want more competition? Your league manager can enable Division Mode, which splits the league into two divisions. Each division has its own season race, and at the end of each season the top players from Division II get promoted while the bottom players from Division I get relegated. The number promoted and relegated is configurable (1–3).

Newly promoted players receive immunity from relegation for their first season, and players who miss a full week move to the front of the relegation line.

Requires at least 6 active players. League managers can enable this from the league settings page.

🔄 Data Reset & Revert

The league manager has the ability to reset various league data if needed. This includes resetting the current season table (clearing all weekly winners), resetting all previous season winners and the season counter, and resetting all-time stats for the entire league or for individual players. Most resets include a revert window so the manager can undo them if needed.
